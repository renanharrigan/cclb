What Is A Land Bank:


The Land Bank is a statutory tool that partners with cities to return tax delinquent dilapidated, and abandon properties to productive use. Your local Land Bank Authority processes applications from interested governmental agencies, businesses and individuals, and conveys the desired properties for public use, or to be reintroduced into the revenue-producing tax rolls.
